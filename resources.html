Trainer prompts and source habits.

Use reliable public sources and separate confirmed facts from forecasts.

Source habit

  • Prefer official institutions and major financial news sources.
  • Check publication date and definitions.
  • Mark assumptions separately from facts.

Class prompts

  • Why does this matter?
  • Who is exposed?
  • Where does it enter the model?
  • When does the view change?
  • How should assumptions move?
