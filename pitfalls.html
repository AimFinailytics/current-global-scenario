Pitfalls

Do not stop at a headline.

Good analysis separates facts, exposure, timing and model impact.

Headline bias

Not every public event has a direct business impact.

Single forecast

Use base, downside and recovery cases instead of one overconfident view.

No trigger

Every scenario needs a clear condition that changes the view.
