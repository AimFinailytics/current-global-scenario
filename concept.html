Core concept

Think in channels, not headlines.

A geopolitical event matters to finance when it changes probability, timing or cash flow.

Energy

Energy cost can affect inflation, pricing power and gross margin.

Trade

Tariffs, regulations and sourcing limits can affect cost, delivery certainty and inventory planning.

Capital

Uncertainty can affect credit cost, discount rates, deal activity and valuation multiples.
